Non Drug Management of High Cholesterol

The following strategies have been shown to reduce "bad cholesterol" in the body and hence improve cardiovascular health:

  • Stop smoking of cigarettes and or do not embark on it
  • Control systemic hypertension (high blood pressure)
  • Embark on a healthy diet such as avoidance of red meat. Eat foods rich in omega-3 fatty acids (fish oils - abundant in salmon, herring, tuna; olive oil and canola oil), nuts (walnuts, pistachios) and fibre rich foods such as fruits, vegetables and beans.
  • Lose weight if overweight or obese
  • Exercise
  • If diabetic, control blood sugar
