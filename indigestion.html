Non Drug Management of Indigestion

Red Signs and Symptoms requiring urgent investigations

When any of the following is present, then there is need for urgent presentation to hospital for further investigations. It is not recommended to embark on the non medical management strategies until more sinister lesions have been ruled out. These include:


  • New onset indigestion in someone 60 yrs and above
  • Evidence of bleeding â€“ vomiting of blood, passage of black stools or overt blood in stools
  • Iron deficiency anaemia
  • Loss of appetite
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Difficulty in swallowing
  • Persistent vomiting
  • History of stomach or oesophageal cancer in first degree relatives

Non Drug Advice

The following are advisable for those that do not have above symptoms

  • Avoid foods rich in sugars, soda, caffeine and alcohol
  • Lose weight, if you are overweight
  • Eat more bananas, melons, yoghurt, green vegetables and oatmeals
  • Avoid foods that trigger symptoms such as cigarettes, caffeine and alcohol
  • Try relaxation exercises
  • Do not eat food 3 hours to bedtime
  • Talk to a health personnel
  • Small regular meals is better than large chunks of meals
